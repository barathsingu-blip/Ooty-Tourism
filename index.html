About Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is one of the most beautiful hill stations in India. Located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, Ooty is famous for its cool climate, lush green tea plantations, lakes, waterfalls, and scenic mountain views.

Tourists from all over India visit Ooty to experience the peaceful atmosphere, ride the famous Nilgiri Mountain Railway toy train, explore botanical gardens, and enjoy boating at Ooty Lake.

Top Tourist Attractions

Ooty Lake

A beautiful lake where visitors enjoy boating and scenic views.

Doddabetta Peak

The highest mountain peak in the Nilgiris with amazing views.

Nilgiri Toy Train

A UNESCO heritage railway passing through tunnels and hills.

Botanical Garden

A garden with rare plants, flowers, and beautiful landscapes.
